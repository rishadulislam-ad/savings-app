Card numbers and CVVs you store in the App are encrypted with AES-256-GCM. The encryption key is generated randomly on first use and stored in iOS Keychain or Android Keystore — the operating system only releases the key to this app. The encrypted card data and the key both stay on your device, and never sync to the cloud or to us. We do not have access to your full card numbers under any circumstances. You are responsible for protecting access to your device and the App's security features (Face ID, Touch ID, PIN).

If you copy a full card number or CVV from the in-app Wallet, the value is automatically wiped from the system clipboard 30 seconds later, or immediately when you switch to another app, to limit clipboard exposure.

7. Cloud Synchronization

Transaction data, budgets, savings goals, travel trips, card metadata (last 4 digits, name, expiry, network only), and account settings sync to Firebase Cloud Firestore to enable cross-device access. Cloud writes use atomic per-element operations so that changes made on one device while another is offline are merged correctly when both reconnect — you will not silently lose entries to write conflicts. You can delete all cloud data at any time from within the App. Firebase App Check (Apple App Attest on iOS, Google Play Integrity on Android, reCAPTCHA v3 on web) gates all backend access.
